Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Narrate collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address, obtained via Clio OAuth authentication.
  • Audio recordings — voice recordings you make within the app, transmitted to Microsoft Azure for transcription. Recordings are not stored after processing.
  • Billing information — payment details are handled by Stripe and are never stored on our servers.
  • Usage data — basic logs of API activity for debugging and service improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To transcribe audio and generate billing narratives.
  • To save time entries to your Clio account on your behalf.
  • To send service-related communications (billing, onboarding, updates).

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services to operate:

  • Upstash — session and token storage.
  • Vercel — application hosting and deployment.
