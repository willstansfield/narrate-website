The problem

Most attorneys write their time entries at the end of the day. From memory.

That means vague narratives, missing detail, and time that gets written off before it ever reaches a client invoice. The billing entry — one of the most important tasks in a law firm — is the one that gets the least attention.

Narrate changes that. By making it faster to bill in the moment than to put it off, attorneys capture more time, with better detail, every day.

How it works

Three steps. Under ten seconds.

1
Dictate

Speak your time entry naturally, the same way you'd describe it to a colleague. No formatting required.

2
Polish

Narrate transcribes your note and rewrites it as a professional, detailed billing narrative — instantly.

3
Save to Clio

Review, adjust the hours if needed, and save directly to the right matter in Clio. Done.

FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need a Clio account to use Narrate?
Yes — Narrate is built specifically for Clio Manage. When you sign up, you'll connect your Clio account and Narrate will pull in your matters automatically. If your firm doesn't use Clio yet, reach out and we can discuss options.
Is my clients' data kept confidential?
Absolutely. Audio recordings are transcribed in real time and immediately discarded — nothing is stored by Narrate. Your billing narratives sync directly to Clio and are never retained on our servers. Your data is never used to train AI models.
How does beta access work?
Beta is completely free — no credit card required. Apply using the form, and we'll add you within one business day. You'll receive an email with your access link and can start billing immediately.
What happens after the beta?
After beta, Narrate moves to a paid pilot at $149 per attorney per month. Beta participants lock in that rate for their first year. We'll give you plenty of notice before any billing begins.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. Narrate is designed to be used on your phone — the most natural place to dictate a time entry right after a call or meeting. It works in mobile Safari and can be added to your home screen for a native app feel.
Can multiple attorneys at my firm use it?
Yes. Each attorney gets their own login and their entries save to Clio under their name. Pricing is per attorney per month.
