Introducing Narrate

Billing narratives. Dictated, polished, done.

Speak your time entry. Narrate turns it into a professional billing narrative and saves it directly to Clio — in seconds.

The problem

Most attorneys write their time entries at the end of the day. From memory.

That means vague narratives, missing detail, and time that gets written off before it ever reaches a client invoice. The billing entry — one of the most important tasks in a law firm — is the one that gets the least attention.

Narrate changes that. By making it faster to bill in the moment than to put it off, attorneys capture more time, with better detail, every day.

How it works

Three steps. Under ten seconds.

1
Dictate

Speak your time entry naturally, the same way you'd describe it to a colleague. No formatting required.

2
Polish

Narrate transcribes your note and rewrites it as a professional, detailed billing narrative — instantly.

3
Save to Clio

Review, adjust the hours if needed, and save directly to the right matter in Clio. Done.

The difference

What changes when billing takes ten seconds.

More time captured

Attorneys bill in the moment, not from memory at 6pm. Less gets forgotten. Less gets written off.

Better narratives

Entries are specific, professional, and client-ready — without the attorney spending time crafting them.

Less time at a desk

End-of-day billing sessions disappear. Attorneys leave when the work is done — not when the entries are.

Clio integration

Built for firms that run on Clio.

Narrate connects directly to your Clio account. Time entries are saved to the right matter, under the right attorney, with the right date — automatically. No copy-pasting, no switching tabs.
